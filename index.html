ABOUT · プロフィール

Hobby artist. Fun first.等身大の音楽 — by Rentaro

連太郎 / Rentaro — also known as Nicart — is a self-produced hobby artist chasing one thing only: the pure joy of making music. He pours his energy into writing lyrics, then uses modern software and virtual instruments to give those words a melody and a voice. Every release happens at his own pace, on his own terms.

Free from commercial rules or genre boundaries, he moves easily between pop, rock and ballads. His lyrics weave English, Japanese and Tagalog into a borderless sound — songs that don't ask to be the centre of attention, but settle naturally into everyday life.

He doesn't chase numbers or charts. He chases Fun — songs that catch your ear and make the day feel a little lighter. That, more than anything, is what keeps the music coming.

商業的な枠組みに囚われず、マイペースに音楽を作り続ける。「Fun（楽しさ）」を最優先に、ジャンルや国境に囚われない自由なセンスで、独自の音楽世界をのびのびと拡大している。
